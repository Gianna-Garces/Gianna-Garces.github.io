FitnessRX

FitnessRX is a full-stack website dedicated to fitness enthusiasts, offering a platform where users can create workouts from over 1,300 exercises. Users are able to tailor to their workout prefrences by selecting their sets and reps as well as the ability to select certain body parts to focus on. Users can interact with other users, edit or delete workouts, modify their profile, and add workouts to their own personal calendar. FitnessRX was made using ExerciseDB and TomTom Map APIs. The platform was built using Springboot, Thymeleaf, JavaScript, jQuery, MySQL, CSS, and HTML resulting in a seamless user experience.

Plantlister

Introducing Plantlister, a comprehensive full-stack website designed exclusively for plant enthusiasts, offering a dynamic platform for buying and selling a diverse range of seeds, seedlings, plants, trees, and more. Think of it as a simplified version of Craigslist specifically tailored to cater to the needs of plant lovers. This collaborative project involved the combined efforts of myself and three other partners, resulting in the creation of a highly functional and user-friendly platform. Together, we successfully developed a seamless system for posting and browsing advertisements. Through our collaborative work, we gained valuable experience in web development, database management, and software testing. Our collective expertise in paired programming, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Spring, MySQL, and jQuery played a crucial role in bringing this plant-focused marketplace to life.

Coffee Project

In close collaboration with a partner, I engaged in the design and development of an engaging web-based coffee website. Our primary objective revolved around optimizing the user experience and interface. Employing dynamic elements sourced from an object array, we creatively showcased a diverse array of coffee names and roast types. Additionally, we implemented a local storage feature that empowered users to conveniently save and retrieve their search results. To further elevate the user experience, we introduced a functionality enabling users to generate new coffee types and roasts. Moreover, we integrated filtering options based on roast type, which users could access either through manual input or selection. This project fortified my skills in paired programming, as well as proficiency in JavaScript, HTML, and CSS.

Weather Map

Silver Screen Dreams

As part of a paired programming project with a collaborative partner, we successfully designed and developed a web-based movie database. Our implementation revolved around the effective utilization of CRUD (create, read, update, delete) functionality to manage dynamically rendered elements. To enhance user interaction, we incorporated modal windows, enabling seamless deletion, addition, and editing of movies. Furthermore, our solution offered the convenience of selecting individual checkboxes for bulk deletion and the ability to modify movie details such as title and year. This project sharpened our skills in paired programming, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and jQuery.
